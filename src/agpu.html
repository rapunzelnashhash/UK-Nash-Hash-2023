YH3 AGPU24

8th-10th March 2024, Bagden Hall Hotel, Wakefield Road, Scissett, Huddersfield HD8 9LE

All the rooms at the hotel are currently booked, but you can attend the runs and Saturday dinner/dance if you arrange your own accommodation. Please use the off-site booking form to book your place on the runs/dinner. This option costs £75

You must arrange your own accommodation. See e.g.:

  • The Woodman Inn, 1 Wakefield Road, Clayton West, Huddersfield HD8 9QB
  • The Foxglove, 36A Penistone Road, Kirkburton, Huddersfield HD8 0PQ
  • Heaps House, 11a Dearneside Rd, Denby Dale, Huddersfield HD8 8TP

The above hotels are recommended by Bagden Hall, there are many others in the area.

The Venue: Bagden Hall Hotel

A beautiful hotel set in 40 acres of woodland, on the A636 Huddersfield to Denby Dale road, just 15 minutes from M1 junctions 38 and 39. Regular buses from Wakefield and Huddersfield train stations.

When: 8-10 March 2024
Theme: Fancy Hats and Waistcoats

Payment requests will be sent out after we have received your booking form.

If you would like to find out more about the hotel you can visit the Bagden Hall website.
