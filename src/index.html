UK Nash Hash 2023 in Yorkshire

Welcome to UK Nash Hash 2023 in Yorkshire Friday 25th August - Monday 28th August

Registration will open on 10 AM, 11th March 2023 GMT. Booking forms will appear here then.

REGISTRATION OPENS IN ONE 3 MINUTES!!!

We can’t wait to welcome you to one of the UK’s most beautiful counties, location of the Yorkshire Dales and North York Moors national parks. Our venue is Askham Bryan College, on the outskirts of the historic city of York.

The theme of the weekend is Vikings, a nod to York’s heritage as a Viking settlement.

The majority of the accommodation is in individual student rooms in the college, as well as some spaces for tents and caravans / camper vans.

What’s included:

  • 3 nights accommodation (Friday, Saturday, Sunday).
  • Breakfast, evening meal and packed lunches on trails
  • 2 days of hashing with a wide selection of trails to suit all tastes
  • Evening entertainment
  • Alcohol and soft drink
  • Goodie bag

Thursday night accommodation is available at extra cost

The Venue

Nash Hash will take place at Askham Bryan College, York.

Please note, dogs are not allowed at all at the venue

Registration

  • Registration will open on 10 AM, 11th March 2023 GMT. Booking forms will appear here then.
  • There will be a waiting list for those who miss the initial allocation. We will get back to you if more places become available.
  • Please select the appropriate form according to your accommodation preference (student room, tent, caravan/camper van).
  • For rooms in college, use one form per person or couple
  • For tent and caravan/camper van, use one form per tent/caravan/camper van and list all its occupants in the form.
  • If you wish to use off site accommodation, please register using the off-site booking form. Note that accommodation in the area may be heavily booked because Nash Hash is the same weekend as York Races.
  • On receipt of your form, you will be sent details of how to pay. You are not confirmed registered until we have received your payment.
  • Registration is only available for the whole event (Red Dress Run is optional extra). We are unable to make places available for part of the weekend.
Prices
  • Single en-suite room £ 240 per person.
  • Single standard (shared bathroom) room £ 230 per person.
  • Twin standard (shared bathroom) room £ 210 per person.
  • Tent £ 195 per person.
  • Camper van/Caravan £ 195 per person.
  • Arranging your own off-site accomodation £ 195 per person.

Children are same price as adults.

Thursday 24th August: Red Dress Run

This is an optional additional event, with accommodation as above at Askham Bryan College. Registration is an option on the main event booking form, but you will be billed separately. Registration for the Red Dress Run is only available to Nash Hash attendees and is limited to 150 people.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q. How do I pay? A. By bank transfer - you will be contacted about payment after registration.
  • Q. Are children allowed? A. Yes. At same price as adults.
  • Q. Are dogs allowed? A. Sorry, no. Dogs are not allowed at the venue.
