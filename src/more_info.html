Yorkshire Hash House Harriers

Yorkshire Hash House Harriers Information

The Hash House Harriers (HHH or H3) is an international group of non-competitive running/walking social clubs, affectionately known as A Drinking Club With A Running Problem.

If you are new to hashing, or to the area, you are most welcome to come and join us. Most hashes include separate trails for runners and walkers. Although directions to YH3 and SH4 runs are included in the Hareline webpage in this website, please contact one of the hashers listed on the contacts page to let them know that you are coming in case of any last minute changes.

Below is a list of some of the hashes in Yorkshire. For the rest of the UK see the directory of UK hashes.
