YH3 AGPU25

31st January - 2nd February 2025, Bagden Hall Hotel, Wakefield Road, Scissett, Huddersfield HD8 9LE

The Venue: Bagden Hall Hotel

A beautiful hotel set in 40 acres of woodland, on the A636 Huddersfield to Denby Dale road, just 15 minutes from M1 junctions 38 and 39. Regular buses from Wakefield and Huddersfield train stations.

When: 31st January - 2nd February 2025
Theme: Pies

Denby Dale has a tradition of baking giant pies to celebrate important occasions. What could be more important than AGPU?

Booking
  • Click here to book a double/twin room
  • Click here to book a single room (limited availability)

If the rooms are full or you would like to find someone to share a room with, you can add your name to the waiting list.

Priority for double and twin rooms will be given to sharing pairs and couples.

  • Prices: Double/twin room £189 per person until 1st December 2024, £199 thereafter
  • Single room £259 per person until 1st December 2024, £269 thereafter

Payment requests will be sent out after we have received your booking form.

If you would like to find out more about the hotel you can visit the Bagden Hall website.

If you wish to stay at the hotel for any additional nights (e.g. Thursday or Sunday), please indicate this on your form. We will notify the hotel, and you will then have to pay the hotel on arrival. The cost of rooms for Sunday and Thursday is £85 for double and £65 for single per night.
